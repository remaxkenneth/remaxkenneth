Casa Sol y Luna

A private hillside property above Playa Los Cerritos with ocean views, three independent casitas, and a pool — set on an 1,801 m² lot.

$1,300,000 USD

Residential Home · Gavilan · Cerritos / El Pescadero · Baja California Sur, Mexico

5 Bedrooms 5 Bathrooms 1,801 m² Lot Three Independent Casitas Pool

Casa Sol y Luna is positioned in the Gavilan hillside above Playa Los Cerritos, offering privacy, elevation, and open Pacific views. The property is arranged as three independent casitas set within native landscaping, creating a layout that works equally well for personal use or hosting guests.

Outdoor living is central to the experience, with generous terraces and a pool designed to take advantage of the surrounding landscape and Baja’s year-round climate. Located minutes from Cerritos Beach and within easy reach of Todos Santos and El Pescadero, the home offers a refined yet relaxed coastal lifestyle.
