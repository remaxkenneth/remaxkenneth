Casa Sol y Luna

5 Bedrooms · 5 Bathrooms · Gavilan above Cerritos / El Pescadero

$1,300,000 USD

Residential Home · Baja California Sur, Mexico

5 Bedrooms 5 Bathrooms 1,801 m² Lot Three Independent Casitas Pool

Where the desert meets the sea, and the sun meets the moon. Casa Sol y Luna is a unique Baja property located above Playa Los Cerritos, positioned between two iconic surf breaks where sunsets paint the sky year-round.

Featuring three independent casitas surrounded by native gardens, the property includes a pool and generous outdoor living areas designed to take full advantage of the setting and views.

Located minutes from Cerritos Beach and within easy reach of Todos Santos and El Pescadero, Casa Sol y Luna offers a relaxed coastal lifestyle in one of Baja California Sur’s most sought-after areas.
