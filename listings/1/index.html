Casa Sol y Luna

A private residence set above Playa Los Cerritos, where desert, ocean, and sky meet in quiet balance.

$1,300,000 USD

Residential Home · Gavilan · Cerritos / El Pescadero · Baja California Sur, Mexico

5 Bedrooms 5 Bathrooms 1,801 m² Lot 245.82 m² Construction Three Independent Casitas Garage · 1 Stall Seller Financing Available

Where the desert meets the sea and the sun meets the moon, Casa Sol y Luna occupies a rare hillside position above Playa Los Cerritos, offering uninterrupted 180-degree ocean views and year-round sunsets over the Pacific.

The property spans an expansive 1,801 m² lot and is thoughtfully designed as three independent casitas surrounded by native gardens and open terraces. At its heart is an endless pool with adjustable current, complemented by a solar-heated pool and multiple outdoor living areas shaded by palapa roofs.

Powered by CFE with a hybrid solar backup system, and equipped with water filtration and modern infrastructure, Casa Sol y Luna offers independence, comfort, and an authentic Baja lifestyle just minutes from Cerritos Beach and two renowned surf breaks.
