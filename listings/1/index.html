Villa Sol y Luna

Residential Villa · Gavilan · Cerritos / El Pescadero · Baja California Sur, Mexico

5 Bedrooms 5 Bathrooms 1,801 m² Lot 245.82 m² Construction Three Independent Casitas Garage · 1 Stall Seller Financing Available

Where the desert meets the sea and the sun meets the moon, Villa Sol y Luna occupies a rare position above Playa Los Cerritos, offering uninterrupted 180-degree ocean views and year-round sunsets over the Pacific. The property spans an expansive 1,800 m² lot and is thoughtfully designed as three independent casitas surrounded by native gardens and open terraces.

At the heart of the property is an endless pool with adjustable current, complemented by a solar-heated pool and multiple outdoor living areas shaded by palapa roofs. The main casita features open living spaces, ocean-view terraces, and an upper-level art studio positioned to capture the full spectrum of Baja’s evening light.

Designed for both independence and comfort, the villa is powered by CFE with a hybrid solar backup system, includes water filtration, spacious garage storage, and modern infrastructure throughout. Located between two renowned surf breaks and minutes from Cerritos Beach, Villa Sol y Luna offers a refined yet authentic Baja lifestyle in one of the region’s most magical settings.
