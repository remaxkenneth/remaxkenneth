Casa Sol y Luna is located in the Gavilan neighborhood above Playa Los Cerritos, within the El Pescadero corridor of Baja California Sur. This elevated setting offers privacy and sweeping views while remaining minutes from one of the region’s most popular swimmable and surf-friendly beaches.

The area is known for its relaxed coastal lifestyle, organic farms, and strong sense of community, with easy access to both Cerritos Beach and the cultural amenities of Todos Santos. The home’s five-bedroom, five-bathroom layout makes it well suited for full-time living, vacation use, or long-term investment.
